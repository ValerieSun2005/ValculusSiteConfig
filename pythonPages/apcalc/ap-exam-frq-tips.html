50 Last-Minute Tips for the AP Calculus Exam

The AP Calculus exams—AB and BC—are each 3 hours and 15 minutes long and are scored on a scale from 1 to 5, where students can expect to earn college credit for scores of 3 or higher. The format of the exams includes two multiple choice (MCQ) sections and two free-response (FRQ) sections. You'll have one hour to answer 30 non-calculator MCQ questions; thirty minutes to answer 15 calculator-active MCQ questions; thirty minutes to answer 2 calculator-active FRQ questions; and one hour to answer 4 non-calculator FRQ questions.
