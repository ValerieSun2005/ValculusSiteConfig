1.3 Exercises — Evaluating Limits Algebraically

For each of exercises 1–13, evaluate the limit if it exists.

\(1.\) \(\ds \lim_{t \to 9} \frac{t^2 - 81}{t - 9}\) \(2.\) \(\ds \lim_{x \to 1} \frac{x^2 + 2x - 3}{x - 1}\) \(3.\) \(\ds \lim_{x \to 3} \frac{x + 3}{x^2 - 9}\)
\(4.\) \(\ds \lim_{\theta \to \pi/2} \frac{1 - \sin^2 \theta}{\cos \theta} \) \(5.\) \(\ds \lim_{\theta \to 0} \frac{\sin 2 \theta}{\sin \theta}\) \(6.\) \(\ds \lim_{\theta \to \pi/4} \frac{\cos 2 \theta}{\cos \theta - \sin \theta}\)
\(7.\) \(\ds \lim_{x \to 4} \frac{\frac{1}{x} - \frac{1}{4}}{x - 4}\) \(8.\) \(\ds \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\frac{2}{x - 2} + 1}{x}\) \(9.\) \(\ds \lim_{t \to 1} \frac{t - 1}{1 - \sqrt t} \)
\(10.\) \(\ds \lim_{x \to 7} \frac{7 - x}{2 - \sqrt{11 - x}}\) \(11.\) \(\ds \lim_{u \to 3} \frac{\sqrt{u - 2} - 1}{2u - 6}\) \(12.\) \(\ds \lim_{x \to 4} \frac{\sqrt{13 - x} - 3}{\sqrt{x} - 2}\)
\(13.\) \(\ds \lim_{x \to 10} \frac{\sqrt{26 - x} - 4}{\sqrt{19 - x} - 3}\)
